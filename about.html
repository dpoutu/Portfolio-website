Background

Here you'll find my background story, how I got started, and what motivates me in my tech journey.

I started my career as a welder for a few years, which is why my
background reflects welding. However, it was not my true passion. I’ve
always been fascinated by the complexities of computers, especially
coding, and the incredible things you can create by giving a few
rulesets to a computer and watching it execute them. What began as a
hobby quickly grew into a passion, and eventually became an obsession.
This led me to pursue a Bachelor's in Computing Systems, where I am
deepening my understanding of programming, networking, and system
design. My education has taught me the value of both precision and
creativity in solving technical challenges efficiently and
effectively.
